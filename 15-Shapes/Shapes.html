DEATH

The physical embodiment of death and main antogonist in the movie Puss in Boots.

Appearance

Death appears as a menacing white wolf in a black hoded cloack.

Purpose

Death collects the souls of those who die or wasted thier life.

Development

Death Spares Puss after he values his life. And reminds him they will meet again.

Representation

Death represents the personification of Puss's fear of losing his lifestyle, which is filled with adventure and unpredictable events.
